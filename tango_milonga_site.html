What is Tango?

Tango is a passionate partner dance that originated in the late 19th century along the border between Argentina and Uruguay. It's characterized by close embrace, improvisation, and the profound connection between partners. A milonga is a social tango dance event where dancers gather to share the floor, typically featuring tandas (sets of songs) separated by cortinas (short musical breaks). It's more than just dancing—it's a conversation without words, an embrace that tells a story.
